    BRAIN GAMES
    THE JURY TRIAL
    GROUP DISCUSSION
    RULES
    Provisional Registration is now open. The link for registration is -LINK

    Rules

    -
    1. NO STRONG LANGUAGE: A certain level of enthusiasm, energy and even passion is welcome.
    2. NO HOSTILITY: Respect other's opinions. Remarks based on gender, race, religion, sexual orientation, or nationality are not allowed. No blame games: discuss the problem, but don't attack the person.
    3. SMART PHONES / LAPTOP POLICY
    3A. PHONES: Should be either switched off or at least set to silent mode and may not be answered unless in case of an emergency or exceptional circumstances.
    3B. LAPTOPS: Laptops, tablets or any other gadgets are strictly not allowed in the GD Hall.
    4. CHALLENGING / DISAGREEING IS WELCOME, BUT RESPECTFULLY: Be free to speak minds without fear or reprisal. No personal attacks 5. NO SIDE CONVERSATIONS: Stay on topic / task.
    6. FOLLOW THE AGENDA: The rules as provided are strictly to be adhered to by the participants.
    7. JUDGEMENT CRITERIA: The participants will be judged on the basis of the logic applied as well as articulation.
    8. TOPIC FOR DISCUSSION: The topics for group discussion will be declared on the day of the event itself.
    9. DECISION OF THE MODERATOR: The decision of the Moderator shall be final, binding and non appealable in case of any discrepancy.
    10. REGISTRATION: The number of people participating will be decided on first come, first serve basis.

    DEBATE
    ENGLISH TURNCOAT DEBATE
    Provisional registration is open. To register, send us a mail at ipu.barthelexfiesta@gmail.com The Rules and Regulations of the Turncoat Debate are as follows:
    Topics: The topic will be released 10 days before the scheduled date of the event .
    Speaking Time: Each speaker will have to speak for 8 minutes on the given topic. The order of changing sides is as follows:
    A. 1 minute - For
    B. 1 minute - Against
    C. 2 minutes - For
    D. 3 minutes - Against
    E. 1 minute – Summary
    Use of foul language not allowed. Decision of the judges will be final and binding upon all participants.
    For queries, Send us a mail at ipu.barthelexfiesta@gmail.com Or call up Karan Gupta, +918447501507
    Provisional registration is open. To register, send us a mail at ipu.barthelexfiesta@gmail.com RULES-
    • This is a team event, each team must comprise of two members; first speaking in favour of the Motion and the second against it. Cross-college teams are allowed.
    • Motion i.e. the Topic for the Debate will be released 10 days before the scheduled date of the Event.
    • Each Speaker will get 3 Minutes of speaking time and an additional 1 minute to conclude (Total time 4 minutes). For using extra time over the given four minutes the speaker will be penalized.
    • No separate marks for interjections although Speakers may voluntarily invite interjections from the audience after their four minutes. Two (2) interjections at maximum or three minutes after the first question whichever is less will be allowed only. No cross questioning.
    • Use of foul language is not allowed.
    • Decision of the judges will be final and binding upon all participants.

    PHOTOGRAPHY COMPETITION
    The online photography competition of IPU - Bar: The Lex Fiesta, Annual Law Fest of USLLS

    Themes:
    - Candid Streets
    - Doors

    Judging Criteria for the preliminary round: 100% weightage will be given to the mass appeal or the number of ‘likes’ on the photo uploaded/posted by the participant on the Official Facebook Page.

    Page link: https://www.facebook.com/IPU-BarThe-Lex-Fiesta-Annual-Law-Fest-of-USLLS-1664392993821346/

    Final Round
    3 photos with maximum likes from each category will be judged by a panel of experienced professionals in the field. ...
    40% weightage will be given to the relevance to the theme, caption, appeal and clarity, while 60% weightage to the exposure, colours/monochrome, post processing, focus, framing, and justification to the subject and overall minute technicalities.

    Rules:

    1. The participants can send only one entry per theme, therefore a total of 2 photos maximum.

    2. All types of cameras are allowed. Excessive post-processing is not allowed (for instance, panoramas, HDRS, filters); please do note that the histogram of the entry sent to us will easily give away the use of any of the unaccepted methods.

    3. Photos should be in JPEG format with an appropriate caption along with Name the Photographer, Name of the College.


    'Like Policy':
    a. The voter or the liker has to 'like' the Facebook Page in order for it to be qualified vote.
    b. Use of any unauthorized software will result in immediate disqualification.

    Important dates: Last date of sending entries is 15th March.

    Results will be announced on the 20th of March, 2016. The best entry will get certificates and a cash prize.
