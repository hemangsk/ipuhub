GENERAL INSTRUCTIONS

General Instructions for participation in various events of
17 th Annual Cultural Festival ‘Anugoonj-2016’
(For all events)
• Judges’ decision will be final and binding in all events.
• Props, dresses, stationery, special equipments etc will not be provided and will not be compensated for as well.
• All the accompanists and participants must have their identity card of their respective institute at time of participation.
No outsider candidate will be allowed for participation in any event.
• The teams should carry an official letter from their respective institute signed by the competent authority mentioning
the names of the participants.
• Late entries will not be allowed
• Information regarding the events will be available at the Help Desks
• Organizers will not be responsible for the security participants’ personal belongings.
• Details for the final registrations will be uploaded after prelims.
• All the final participants are required to get themselves registered; else, they will not be entertained.
• All participants are required to be on time else their registration will stand cancelled.
• Entry of students/ participants of USS/affiliated institutes will be allowed after checking of their identity cards during
the festival Anugoonj-2016 at Dwarka Campus of the University only and required to produce their College Identity Card
on demand at any time.
• All participants/audience are advised to use public conveyance for reaching the University Campus during the Festival.
• PRELIMINARY ROUNDS WILL BE HELD IN EACH ZONE as notified on the University’s website.
• All zonal results must be submitted in proper format by the zonal coordinators to the Directorate of Students’ Welfare,
GGSIP University, Dwarka latest by 4th February, 2016.1. M USIC

Music 
(A) Classical Vocal solo (Hindustani & Carnatic)
•Classical Hindustani and Carnatic only.
•Number of participants is 1.
•Maximum number of accompanists is 2.
•Sufficient thought and care must be exercised in the choice of raga and composition.
•Judgment will be based on the qualities like swara, taal, selection of raga, composition
and general impression.
• Max. Time Limit : 5+ 2 minutes

(B) Light Vocal Music (Indian)
•Individual participation.
•Two accompanists/instrumentalists are allowed.
•Only non-film songs / geet / ghazal / bhajan / shabad and abhangas can be presented.
•Time Limit : 4+2 minutes

(C) Western Vocal Solo
•Number of participants is one.
•Two accompanists/instrumentalists allowed.
•Time limit : 4+2 minutes
•Judgment will be based on the qualities like selection of song, tempo, rhythm, range and melody.

(D) Group Song (Indian)
•Minimum number of participants in a team is 6, and maximum 12.
•Maximum of 4 accompanists allowed.
•Time Limit: 8 + 2 minutes (including stage set up)
•Judgment will be based on quality of singing, coordination and not on makeup, costume or actions of the team.
•No film songs allowed.

(E) Group Song (western)
• Minimum number of participants in a team is 6, and maximum 12.
• Maximum of 4 instruments/ accompanists allowed
• Time Limit: 8+2 minutes (including stage set up)
• Judgment will be based on quality of singing only and not on makeup, costume or actions of the team.
• The group songs should be in English only
General:
· All the accompanists/instrumentalists and participants must be of the same college. No outsiders
allowed.
· The teams should carry an official letter from their respective institute signed by the competent
authority mentioning the names of the participants.
· No recorded vocals will be allowed.

DANCE 
A. Folk Dance
• Minimum of 8 participants, maximum 16
(including 2 accompanists)
• Time limit : 6 + 4 minutes
• Judgment will be based on rhythm,
formation,
synchronization,
expressions,
costumes, makeup, sets, and overall effects.
• No bollywood music allowed.
• Restriction to one particular folk dance form.
• Props allowed.
• Live music is not allowed.


B. Choreography
• Minimum of 8 participants, maximum 16
(including 2 accompanists)
• Time Limit: 7 + 5 minutes (including stage
set up time).
• Extra 1 minute will be given for explaining
the theme.
• No lyrics allowed, only instrument. Some
words may be allowed.
• Props allowed.
• Folk dances not allowed.
• The theme should have an original
expression through music and movement.

C. Classical Dance
• One participant and Three accompanists allowed
• Time Limit: 6 + 4 minutes
• Judgment will be based on rhythm, formation,
synchronization, expressions, costumes, make up,
sets, and overall effects.
• No bollywood song allowed.
• Restriction to one particular classical dance form.

D. “Nach Baliye”
• Two participants per team( 1 Boy/ 1 Girl)
• Props allowed.
• Time Limit: 6 + 2 minutes.

E. “Footloose”
• Minimum of 8 participants, maximum 15.
• Time Limit: 10 + 2 minutes
• Props allowed.

F. “Street Dance”
• Minimum of 8 participants,
(including 2 accompanists).
• Time limit = 10+2 minutes
• Only western music allowed.
• Props allowed. 

General:
· All the accompanists and participants must be of same college. No outsiders allowed.
· The teams should carry an official letter from their respective institute signed by the competent
authority mentioning the names of the participants.
· Participants/teams will have to bring the props on their own. No props will be provided by the
managing staff.
· Negative marks for exceeding time limit.
· Each team participating in the event should have their song/music in a pen drive and at least 2 CDs
of the same.
· Participants are advised to reach the venue for respective events 1 hour prior to the commencement of
the event for sound check.
· In case of technical faults, judges results will be final and binding.
· Participants are advised not to choose indecent songs or costumes for the performance, otherwise
they may be disqualified from the event.

Battle of Bands 
1. Maximum of 10 and minimum of 4 members per team.
2. Each band can perform eastern /western or both.
3. Time limit: 20 minutes (5 minutes setup time + 15 minutes playing time)
5% of total marks will be deducted for every extra minute.
4. Use of pre recorded music (e.g. sequences, keyboard rhythm,
guitar loops) will result in disqualification.
5. No instrument will be provided by the organizer.
